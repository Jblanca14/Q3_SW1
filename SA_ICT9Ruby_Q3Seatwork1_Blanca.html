Synopsis:

“Bocchi The Rock” is an anime series that premiered in October, 9 of 2022 consisting of 12 episodes. This series is about a high schooler named Hitori "Bocchi" Gotou, a socially awkward guitar player who dreams of playing in a band. On a fateful day, Bocchi meets the drummer Nijika Ijichi, who invites her to join Kessoku Band which Ryo Yamada is also a part of, she plays bass. They were looking for a new guitarist since their last guitarist, fled before their first show. Bocchi and her bandmates, dedicate their lives to becoming better musicians and making the most of their brief high school years.

Characters:


Hitori "Bocchi" Gotou

A socially anxious high schooler who is dedicated to playing the guitar and spends three years mastering the instrument. She also makes videos on the internet with the alias of “guitarhero” under the suggestion of her father. Shes in the same high school as her other bandmate Kita

Ryo Yamada

The bass player of the band. The first person that was approached by Nijika to join Kessoku band. Is classmates with Nijika and a very cool and stoic girl who comes from a wealthy family. Even though her family is wealthy and gives her an allowance she usually spends it all on instruments leaving her with not enough money for food which is referenced multiple times in the series

Nijika Ijichi

The drummer. The one who started the band, is the sister of the owner of “Starry”, the live house they play in. She is classmates with Ryo and is a very energetic and outgoing person. Although she is the fun and always smiling she has a sad backstory having her mother die when she was just a child, we learn later on in the series that her sister opened Starry for her since she saw how much Nijika loved bands and live music.

Ikuyo Kita

The “Runaway Guitarist” that fled before their first show. Shes in the same high school with Hitori and like Nijika she is also very energetic and social. She is the popular girl in school. It is revealed that she didn't know how to play guitar and only said she did to join the band and get closer to her crush Ryo. She rejoins the band and gets taught by Hitori how to play guitar.

Seika Ijichi

"The sister of Nijika and owner of “Starry” a live house that the band plays in. She is also the unofficial manager of “Kessoku band” and lets them play and hang out in the live house
